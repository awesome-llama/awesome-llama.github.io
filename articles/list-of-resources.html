List of Resources

I've compiled a list of free resources for a wide variety of use-cases. Many offer both textures and 3D models so I haven't separated them into categories. Please double-check the licenses of these sites before using them, what I've listed may not apply to everything or I may have made a mistake. The list is roughly ordered by the overall quality and experience of each, beginning with the most permissible licenses.
